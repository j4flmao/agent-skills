# Automated Market Maker (AMM) Mathematical Models
## 1. Constant Product Formula
The core of many AMMs (like Uniswap V2) is the constant product formula:
$$ x * y = k $$

Where:
- $x$ is the reserve of token A
- $y$ is the reserve of token B
- $k$ is the constant product

### Pricing Derivative
The marginal price of token A in terms of token B is:
$$ P_A = \frac{y}{x} $$
