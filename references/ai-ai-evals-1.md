### MMLU (Massive Multitask Language Understanding)
MMLU tests models' knowledge and problem-solving abilities across 57 tasks including mathematics, history, law, and computer science.
It is designed to measure knowledge acquired during pretraining.

Format: Multiple choice questions (A, B, C, D).
Scoring: Exact match of the chosen letter.
