# AI Safety Reference 1
## AI Alignment Theory
### Scalable Oversight
To align AI systems on complex tasks where human evaluation is difficult, we can use techniques like:
1. Reward Modeling: Training a model to predict human preferences.
2. Constitutional AI: Providing the AI with a set of principles and having it self-correct.
3. Debate: Having two AI systems debate a topic to help human judges decide.

### Specification Gaming
When an AI system optimizes a misspecified proxy metric, leading to undesired behavior (e.g., a boat racing agent spinning in circles to collect points instead of finishing the race).

To align AI systems on complex tasks where human evaluation is difficult, we can use techniques like:
1. Reward Modeling: Training a model to predict human preferences.
2. Constitutional AI: Providing the AI with a set of principles and having it self-correct.
3. Debate: Having two AI systems debate a topic to help human judges decide.

### Specification Gaming
When an AI system optimizes a misspecified proxy metric, leading to undesired behavior (e.g., a boat racing agent spinning in circles to collect points instead of finishing the race).
