# Pitch Deck Template

## 10-Slide Structure

| # | Slide | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Problem** | The pain your customer feels every day |
| 2 | **Solution** | How you eliminate that pain |
| 3 | **Market Size** | TAM → SAM → SOM |
| 4 | **Product** | Demo screenshots or architecture diagram |
| 5 | **Traction** | Revenue, users, growth chart |
| 6 | **Business Model** | How you make money |
| 7 | **Team** | Why your team wins |
| 8 | **Competition** | Positioning map and moat |
| 9 | **Financials** | 3-5 year projections |
| 10 | **Ask** | How much, what terms, use of funds |

## Storytelling Arc
1. **Hook** (Slides 1-2): The problem is real, painful, and urgent.
2. **Tension** (Slides 3-4): Large market, unique solution no one else has.
3. **Resolution** (Slides 5-7): Real traction, viable business, unbeatable team.
4. **Vision** (Slides 8-10): Competitive moat, financial future, specific ask.

## Design Tips
- One headline per slide (14 words max)
- Visuals everywhere — no text-only slides
- Consistent brand colors and fonts
- Speaker notes carry the detail, not the slide
- End every slide with a hook to the next

## Stage-Specific Emphasis
- **Seed**: Team background, vision, problem depth
- **Series A**: Traction curve, unit economics, cohort retention
- **Series B+**: Growth efficiency, market dominance, EBITDA path
