# Pitch Deck Templates

Additional templates for specific pitch scenarios and formats.

## Template 1: One-Pager (Pre-Meeting Leave-Behind)

A single-page summary for initial outreach and follow-up.

```markdown
# One-Pager: {Company Name}

## Headline
{One sentence that makes the reader want to learn more.}

## The Problem
{The specific pain: who feels it, how bad is it, why now?}

## The Solution
{How the product eliminates the pain in one memorable sentence.}

## Why Now
{Market timing: technology shift, regulation change, behavior shift.}

## Market Opportunity
| TAM | SAM | SOM |
|-----|-----|-----|
| ${n}B | ${n}M | ${n}M |

## Traction Highlights
- {Metric 1}: {Number} ({Growth rate})
- {Metric 2}: {Number} ({Growth rate})
- {Metric 3}: {Number}

## Business Model
{pricing model, unit economics, target customer}

## The Team
{Names, previous companies, relevant expertise}

## Ask
${n} {instrument type} at ${n} valuation for {n} months runway
```

## Template 2: Product-Led Growth Deck

Tailored for SaaS products with self-serve acquisition.

| Slide | Focus | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Problem | Users waste time on setup-heavy tools |
| 2 | Solution | Sign up, invite team, start working in 2 minutes |
| 3 | Viral Loop | Each new user invites 3 team members on average |
| 4 | Product Tour | 3 screenshots showing the aha moment |
| 5 | Growth Metrics | DAU/MAU ratio, viral coefficient, funnel conversion |
| 6 | Monetization | Free tier → usage limits → paid plans |
| 7 | Unit Economics | CAC < $50, LTV > $500, payback < 3 months |
| 8 | Team | Product-led growth experience, domain expertise |
| 9 | Competition | Feature comparison vs enterprise incumbents |
| 10 | Ask | Growth marketing hires, self-serve infrastructure |

## Template 3: Enterprise Sales Deck

Tailored for B2B products with sales-led motion.

| Slide | Focus | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Problem | Current solution costs the enterprise $X/year in lost productivity |
| 2 | Cost Analysis | Quantified ROI: customer saves $Y per year with our solution |
| 3 | Solution | Enterprise-grade platform with SOC 2, SSO, audit logs |
| 4 | Security & Compliance | Certifications, data residency, encryption |
| 5 | Integration | Works with existing stack (Salesforce, Slack, Jira, Okta) |
| 6 | Case Study | Fortune 500 customer: implemented in 2 weeks, 300% ROI |
| 7 | Enterprise Features | RBAC, audit trail, custom workflows, SLA guarantees |
| 8 | Implementation Plan | 4-week deployment, dedicated CSM, training included |
| 9 | Pricing | Annual contract, per-seat pricing with volume discounts |
| 10 | Ask | Enterprise pilot: 50 seats, 3-month proof of concept |

## Template 4: Product Update / Series B+ Deck

Tailored for later-stage companies with existing revenue.

| Slide | Focus | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Mission & Vision | Why we exist, where we are going |
| 2 | Market Leadership | #1 in category, fastest growing, most loved |
| 3 | Growth Efficiency | Magic number, payback period, LTV/CAC trend |
| 4 | Product Evolution | What we shipped last year, what's coming |
| 5 | Platform Strategy | API, ecosystem, partnerships expanding the moat |
| 6 | Revenue Breakdown | Segment, product line, geography |
| 7 | Customer Success | Logo retention > 95%, net revenue retention > 120% |
| 8 | Team Scaling | Leadership hires, culture, remote team |
| 9 | Financial Model | 5-year projection, path to profitability |
| 10 | Ask | Scale go-to-market, expand internationally, M&A |

## Template 5: Pre-Seed / Idea Stage Deck

Tailored for founders with no product and no revenue.

| Slide | Focus | Key Message |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Personal Story | Why we are building this (founder pain) |
| 2 | Problem Validation | 50 user interviews, all said this is painful |
| 3 | Market Research | Industry reports, analyst quotes, market trends |
| 4 | Solution Concept | Wireframe or mockup showing the core idea |
| 5 | Competitive Landscape | Why existing solutions fail (feature gap matrix) |
| 6 | Business Model Hypothesis | How we plan to monetize (validated with 10 prospects) |
| 7 | Go-to-Market Plan | Community-first, build in public, PLG motion |
| 8 | Founder-Market Fit | Why this team is uniquely qualified |
| 9 | Roadmap | Build → launch → iterate → scale milestones |
| 10 | Ask | $500K seed, 18 months runway, first 3 hires |

## Visual Design Guidelines

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Font | Sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, Helvetica Neue) |
| Font sizes | Title: 36-48pt, Body: 18-24pt, Footer: 12-14pt |
| Colors | 3 max: primary, accent, neutral |
| Images | Full-bleed, high resolution, consistent style |
| Data charts | Clean line/bar charts, no 3D or pie charts |
| Logos | Customer logos, partner logos, press logos |
| Consistency | Same layout structure across all slides |
