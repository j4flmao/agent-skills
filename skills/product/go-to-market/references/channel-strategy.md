# Channel Strategy

## Channel Types

### Product-Led Channels
```
In-App:
  — Announcement banners (targeted by segment)
  — Feature tooltips and highlights
  — Checklist or progress indicators
  Best for: All launch tiers
  Metrics: Click-through rate, feature adoption

Email:
  — New feature announcements
  — Personalized usage tips
  — Re-engagement campaigns
  -– Launch sequences (drip campaigns)
  Best for: T1 and T2 launches
  Metrics: Open rate, click rate, conversion

Changelog:
  — In-app changelog panel
  — Public changelog page
  — RSS/email subscription for updates
  Best for: T3 launches, continuous updates
  Metrics: View rate, subscriber growth
```

### Content Channels
```
Blog:
  — Product launch posts (T1/T2)
  — Feature deep dives (T2)
  — Use cases and best practices
  Best for: Long-form communication, SEO
  Metrics: Traffic, time on page, signups from blog

Documentation:
  — Feature guides and tutorials
  — API reference updates
  — Video walkthroughs
  Best for: All launch tiers
  Metrics: Doc views, search queries, time-to-activation

Webinars:
  — Product launch demos (T1)
  — Feature workshops (T2)
  — Customer stories (ongoing)
  Best for: T1 launches, deep engagement
  Metrics: Registrations, attendance, replay views
```

### Sales-Led Channels
```
Sales Enablement:
  — Battle cards (competitor comparison)
  — Demo scripts updated for new features
  — Case studies and ROI calculator
  Best for: T1 launches, enterprise features
  Metrics: Sales team adoption, win rate

Partnerships:
  — Co-marketing with partners
  — Partner newsletter inclusion
  — Integration announcements
  Best for: T1 launches with ecosystem impact
  Metrics: Partner-sourced leads, co-marketing impressions
```

## Channel Selection

### By Customer Segment
```
Consumer / Prosumer:
  Primary: In-app, email, social
  Secondary: Content, community
  Focus: Self-serve, high volume

SMB:
  Primary: Email, content, sales
  Secondary: In-app, partnerships
  Focus: Low-touch, automated

Enterprise:
  Primary: Sales, content, events
  Secondary: Email, partnerships
  Focus: High-touch, relationship
```

### By Launch Tier
```
T1: All channels coordinated
  — Email blast + in-app announcement + blog + press + sales
  — Multi-touch, high visibility

T2: Targeted channels
  — Email (segmented) + in-app (targeted) + blog
  — Medium visibility, relevant audience

T3: Light-touch channels
  — In-app notification + changelog
  — Low visibility, minimal effort
```

## Execution Playbooks

### Email Campaign (T1 Launch)
```
Day -7: Teaser email (coming soon)
Day 0: Launch announcement
Day 3: Feature deep dive
Day 7: Customer story / use case
Day 14: "Did you know?" tips email
Day 30: Adoption check-in
```

### In-App Announcement (T2 Launch)
```
Step 1: Banner on dashboard (first visit after launch)
Step 2: Tooltip on new feature location
Step 3: Feature card on empty states
Step 4: Optional walkthrough (if complex)
Step 5: Dismiss after engagement or 3 visits
```

## Channel Metrics

### Performance Tracking
```
Channel           | Reach    | Conversion | Cost
Email             | High     | Medium     | Low
In-app            | High     | High       | Low
Blog / Content    | Medium   | Medium     | Medium
Sales             | Low      | High       | High
Press / PR        | Medium   | Low        | Medium
Paid Ads          | Variable | Variable   | High
```

### Attribution
```
First touch: Which channel drove initial awareness
Last touch: Which channel drove conversion
Multi-touch: Contribution of all channels
Use UTM parameters for all external links
Track in-app source parameters for internal links
```
