# Brand Guidelines Structure

## Document Template
1. **Brand Overview** — Mission, vision, values, brand story
2. **Logo** — Primary, secondary, icon, clear space, minimum size, incorrect usage
3. **Color** — Primary palette, secondary palette, neutral, semantic, accessibility
4. **Typography** — Primary typeface, secondary typeface, hierarchy, web fonts
5. **Voice & Tone** — Brand personality, writing principles, do/don't examples
6. **Imagery** — Photography style, illustration style, iconography, stock photo guidelines
7. **Applications** — Stationery, presentations, social media, swag, advertising
8. **Examples** — Correct and incorrect application examples
