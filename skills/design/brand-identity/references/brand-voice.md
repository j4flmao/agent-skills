# Brand Voice Guide

## Voice Dimensions
| Dimension | Scale |
|-----------|-------|
| Funny vs Serious | Where on the humor spectrum |
| Formal vs Casual | Level of formality |
| Respectful vs Irreverent | Adherence to conventions |
| Enthusiastic vs Matter-of-fact | Emotional intensity |

## Voice Examples
| Brand | Voice | Example Phrasing |
|-------|-------|------------------|
| Mailchimp | Friendly, playful, clear | "Create captivating emails" |
| Apple | Minimal, aspirational, confident | "It just works" |
| Slack | Conversational, helpful, human | "Make work simpler, more pleasant" |
| Stripe | Technical, precise, trustworthy | "Payments infrastructure for the internet" |
