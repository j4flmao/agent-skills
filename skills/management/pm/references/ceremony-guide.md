# Agile Ceremony Guide

## Sprint Planning (2 hours for 2-week sprint)
**Attendees**: PM, Dev lead, QA lead, team
**Output**: Sprint goal + committed stories + task breakdown

```
1. Capacity calculation: 5 devs × 10 days × 6h × 0.7 focus = 210h
2. Story selection: top priority that fits within capacity
3. Task breakdown: each story → tasks with hour estimates
4. Sprint goal: "Complete user onboarding with MFA"
```

## Daily Standup (15 minutes)
**Attendees**: Whole team
**Format**: What I did yesterday / What I'll do today / Blockers
**Rule**: No problem-solving during standup — parking lot for after

## Backlog Refinement (1 hour/week)
**Attendees**: PM, BA, Dev lead, QA lead
**Output**: Refined stories with acceptance criteria, estimated

## Sprint Review (1 hour)
**Attendees**: Team + stakeholders
**Format**: Demo completed work, discuss what changed, review metrics

## Retrospective (1 hour)
**Attendees**: Team only (no stakeholders)
**Format**: What went well / What went wrong / What to improve
**Output**: Action items with owners and deadlines
