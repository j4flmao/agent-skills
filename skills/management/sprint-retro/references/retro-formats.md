# Retro Formats

## Start/Stop/Continue
| Start | Stop | Continue |
|---|---|---|
| [new practices to adopt] | [harmful practices to drop] | [good practices to keep] |

Best for: Balanced sprints, new teams. Simple and action-oriented.

## 4Ls (Liked / Learned / Lacked / Longed For)
| Liked | Learned | Lacked | Longed For |
|---|---|---|---|
| [positive experiences] | [new insights] | [missing elements] | [desired changes] |

Best for: Deep reflection, experienced teams. Uncovers root causes.

## Sailboat
| Wind (helps) | Anchor (slows) | Rocks (risks) | Iceberg (hidden danger) |
|---|---|---|---|
| [tailwinds] | [blockers] | [known risks] | [unknown unknowns] |

Best for: Metaphor-driven discussion, complex projects, risk awareness.

## Mad / Sad / Glad
| Mad | Sad | Glad |
|---|---|---|
| [frustrations] | [disappointments] | [positive moments] |

Best for: Emotional safety, team building, low-trust environments.

## Timebox Guide
1. **5 min** — Individual silent writing
2. **10 min** — Round-robin sharing (1 item per person per round)
3. **15 min** — Discussion and affinity grouping
4. **10 min** — Dot voting, top 3 action items
5. **5 min** — Retro retro (rate the retro itself)
