# Platform Adoption Strategy

## Adoption Phases
| Phase | Goal | Metric |
|-------|------|--------|
| Awareness | Devs know platform exists | % devs who visited portal |
| Trial | Devs try a golden path | % devs who scaffolded a project |
| Adoption | Devs use platform regularly | % new projects on platform |
| Advocacy | Devs recommend platform | NPS score, internal referrals |

## Measuring Developer Experience
- Time-to-production: from commit to running in production
- Cognitive load: how many tools/docs devs need to understand
- Friction log: track every time a dev hits a blocker
- Platform NPS: quarterly developer satisfaction survey

## Champion Program
- Identify 2-3 champions per team
- Champions get early access to new features
- Champions provide feedback and train their teams
- Monthly platform community meeting
